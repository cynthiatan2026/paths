About This Resource

This website is an independently developed supplementary resource created to help staff and students better understand the educational and progression pathways offered by the different schools.

The information is simplified, reorganised and presented in a clearer format to support pathway discussions and course advising.

Important Disclaimer

This is not an official Nanyang Polytechnic website and should not replace official course, admission or progression information.

For the most accurate and updated information, users should refer to the official Nanyang Polytechnic website or seek clarification from the relevant school’s course management team.

The content on this website does not represent official Nanyang Polytechnic policies, decisions or guarantees.

Visit the Official NYP Website

Sources and Accuracy

The information on this website is simplified and organised from publicly available Nanyang Polytechnic webpages and input provided by relevant stakeholders.

Every effort is made to present the information clearly and accurately. However, course structures, admission requirements, progression opportunities and educational pathways may change.

Users should verify important academic, admissions, course-selection and progression information through official Nanyang Polytechnic sources or the relevant school’s course management team.

How to Use This Resource

This resource is intended to support understanding and discussion. It may help staff and students identify possible pathways, related disciplines and questions that should be clarified.

It should not be treated as an official assessment of eligibility, admission outcome or guaranteed progression route.

Information That May Change

Course details, module structures, entry requirements, progression pathways and university admission requirements may change over time.

Please check the official source before making important academic, admissions or progression decisions.
